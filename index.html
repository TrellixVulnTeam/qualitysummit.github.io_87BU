About Us

Quality Summit is a modern training program to prepare anyone to enter the Software Testing industry. With over a decade of combined experience in software quality assurance, teaching, software development, and Agile coaching, we have the know-how to help you level up your Software Testing skills.

Our program is online and designed to give you real-world experience. We offer a combination of live classes, self-guided lessons, and 1:1 coaching.
